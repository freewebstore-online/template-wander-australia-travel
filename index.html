Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit Australia?

Australia is a year-round destination! Summer (Dec-Feb) is perfect for southern beaches, while the dry season (Apr-Sep) is ideal for the tropical north and the outback.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Absolutely. We work closely with all our tour operators and accommodation partners to ensure any dietary needs are met. Just let us know during the planning phase.

What's included in the tour packages?

Each package is different, but they typically include accommodation, specified tours and activities, some meals, and ground transport. We'll provide a detailed inclusion list with your itinerary.
